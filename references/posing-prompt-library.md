# Posing & Hand-Gesture Prompt Library (for video)

Researched fashion-posing phrasing that reads well in image-to-video (Kling 3.0 best for hands).
Combine with identity/wardrobe lock + camera direction. Kling renders continuous motion — for
"fast-paced" add *rapid succession of poses, snappy rhythm, fast push-in/whip-pan*.

## Standing / power poses
- contrapposto stance, weight on one leg, hip slightly tilted
- one hand on hip, strong posture, confident expression
- both hands on hips, strong stance, high-fashion energy
- arms crossed, chin raised, editorial attitude
- hands in pockets, relaxed posture, casual editorial
- body turned away, looking back over the shoulder, high fashion
- one arm behind back, elegant minimalist posture

## Movement
- walking toward camera, mid-stride, confident runway energy
- turning while walking, fabric/blazer flowing, dynamic movement
- sharp half-spin, jacket flaring, quick pivot and pose
- walking away, looking back, cinematic
- step back into a wide stance, re-set

## Hand-to-face / beauty (great for eyewear)
- fingertips touch and adjust the temple/bridge of the glasses  ← ties pose to eyewear product
- running a hand softly through the side of the hair
- fingers grazing the jaw / light touch near the cheekbone
- framing the face with both hands
- fingers touching lips, soft expression, beauty editorial
- chin tilts up and down, clean specular highlights tracing the frame

## Framing recipes
- **Full-body posing:** start wide, head-to-toe → body poses read.
- **Three-quarter (waist up):** hand-to-face gestures + upper-body posing.
- **Mix:** open full-body, slow push-in to three-quarter for the hand/eyewear beats (most variety).

## Camera vocabulary (from the seedance camera-bible)
shot types EWS/WS/MWS/MS/MCU/CU/ECU; moves static / slow dolly in-out / pan / tilt / slow orbit /
tracking / subtle handheld / whip pan / crash-zoom; lenses 24/35/50/85/100mm; Kelvin 2700K warm →
5600K daylight → 7000K cool; add 5 sensory/atmosphere words for depth.

## Guardrail tail (append to every posing prompt)
> Keep the [wardrobe], face identity IDENTICAL to the reference, lenses/product unchanged,
> smooth realistic body mechanics and natural finger movement, no distortion.
